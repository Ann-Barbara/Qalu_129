Milestone 3 – Critique + Theory Comparison

 Evidence Critique
- Small sample sizes limit generalizability  
- Self-report measures may introduce bias  
- Confounds such as social engagement and sleep quality not always controlled  

Competing Explanations
A – Physiological: Neurochemical changes, neurogenesis, mood improvement independent of social context  
B – Psychosocial: Group support, self-efficacy, behavioral activation; explains variability in outcomes  

What Would Change My Mind?
1. Large RCTs showing no effect of exercise on depression  
2. Psychosocial interventions alone producing equal improvements  
3. Biological analyses showing no neurochemical changes post-exercise
