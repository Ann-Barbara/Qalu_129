Topic
Examining How Exercise Can Help Young Adults with Depression: A Qualitative Study on Physical Activity and Mental Health

 Rationale
Depression is a common mental health issue among university students. Exercise is proposed as a potential intervention due to physiological and psychosocial benefits. Understanding its effects can inform health promotion strategies on campus.
